FAQ

Questions clients ask
before booking.

Do I need prior DeFi experience to book a session?
+

No. Sessions are tailored to your current level. Whether you are completely new to DeFi or already managing positions across multiple protocols, the session is structured around where you are and where you want to go. The intake form helps us prepare accordingly so no time is wasted during the call.

What exactly happens during a session?
+

Each session is a focused 60-minute 1:1 video call. We work through your specific situation — whether that is building a strategy, evaluating a protocol, or setting up your execution process. There is no generic presentation. Within 24 hours after the call you receive a written plan with clear rules and next steps you can act on immediately.

Is this financial advice? Are you a registered advisor?
+

No. All consulting is educational and informational in nature. We help you understand DeFi mechanics, evaluate protocols, and build your own risk frameworks — but final decisions are always yours. We do not manage funds, execute trades on your behalf, or provide regulated financial advice. You retain full control at all times.

How do I pay? Can I pay in crypto?
+

Yes. We accept credit and debit cards via Stripe, ACH bank transfer, PayPal, and crypto including USDC, ETH, BTC, and ADA. After you submit the booking form, you will receive a confirmation with payment instructions for your preferred method. Payment is required to confirm your session slot.

What is the difference between the Clarity Session and the Strategy Sprint?
+

The Clarity Session is ideal if you have a specific question, a protocol to evaluate, or want to pressure-test an idea in a single focused call. The Strategy Sprint is for clients who want comprehensive, ongoing work across 10 sessions — building a full risk framework, deep protocol analysis, and a complete execution playbook over time. If you are unsure, start with the Clarity Session. Pricing for all engagements is shared upon inquiry.

Can I reschedule or cancel my session?
+

Yes. You can reschedule at no cost up to 24 hours before your session. Cancellations made within 24 hours of the session are non-refundable. No-shows forfeit the session fee. For the Strategy Sprint and Executive packages, individual sessions within the package can be rescheduled with 24 hours notice.

Are sessions available in Spanish?
+

Yes. All sessions can be conducted fully in Spanish or English — your choice. Simply indicate your language preference in the booking form and the session will be delivered accordingly. Written deliverables are also available in your preferred language.
